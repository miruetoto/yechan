np.unravel_index

- 사용법: numpy.unravel_index(indices, shape, order='C')

- 예제1

X=np.array([[6,5,4],[3,2,1]])
X

array([[6, 5, 4],
       [3, 2, 1]])
np.argmin(X)

5
np.unravel_index(5,X.shape)

(1, 2)
X[1,2]

1

np.ravel_multi_index

- 사용법: numpy.ravel_multi_index(multi_index, dims, mode='raise', order='C')

- 예제2

np.ravel_multi_index((1,2),X.shape)

5
